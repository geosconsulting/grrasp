Dynamic Data Visualization


In the system we have developed a tool to allow you to filter the data calculated in the process of building scenarios using maximum and minimum limits of monetary damage. In Figure 26 is possible to gauge the impact of the blackout that hit Italy in 2003. Applying a lower and upper threshold with damage values of 10 and 17 million Euros the tool displays only the southern regions, as were those with a known time of hospitalization increased and consequently a greater economic damage
